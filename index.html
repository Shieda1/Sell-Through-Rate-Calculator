What is the sell through rate calculator?
The sell through rate calculator is a tool that measures a metric that can be used to assess the inventory management of a business.
How to calculate sell through rate?
To calculate sell through rate you should use the following formula:
sell through rate = (number of units sold / number of units received) * 100
